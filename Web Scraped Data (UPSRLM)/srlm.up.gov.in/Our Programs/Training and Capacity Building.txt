The focus of program will be given on organizing the rural women in to self-sustained institutions in form of SHGs, VOs, Clusters and Federations which can leverage financial and livelihood resources and contribute to their sustainable development. In order to achieve this milestone, it requires building the skills set and capacity of the poor and the capacity building of all stakeholders such as community cadre, project staff community institutions and their representatives, PRIs, Bankers, Government Departments and NGOs.

The Overall Objective of Training and Capacity Building are Given Below:
The overall capacity building strategy is to working towards enhancement of knowledge, skill upgradation and change in behavior with all the attitudinal/behavioral change with the target community, staff and all the stakeholders.
Capacity enhancement of all staffs of UP State Rural Livelihood Mission, who are direct responsible for the effective implementation of the project.
Capacity building of SHG Members, representatives, community institutions like VOs, cluster and federations, which are the main actors to address poverty.
Building the capacity of community cadre such as Samuh Sakhi, internal CRPs, Book Keeper, Bank Mitras, etc. Which are the key instrumental in the expansion of the project and capacity building of community institutions.
Capacity building of line department staff, bankers, PRI representatives, associated NGOs and others stakeholders to able to perform their expected roles as desired by the project.
