Janhit Guarantee Adhiniyam
The Uttar Pradesh Janhit Guarantee Adhiniyam, also known as the Public Services Guarantee Act, is an initiative aimed at ensuring timely delivery of essential public services to citizens. This act is designed to promote transparency, accountability, and efficiency in the delivery of government services.

Objectives
Timely Service Delivery: To ensure that citizens receive public services within a specified time .
Transparency and Accountability: To hold government departments and officials accountable for delays or denial of services.
Redressal Mechanism: To provide a structured grievance redressal system for citizens whose services are delayed or denied.
Key Features
List of Guaranteed Services: The act specifies various services under different departments, including social welfare, rural development, education, and health, that are guaranteed to be provided within the prescribed time .
Time-bound Services: Each service has a clearly defined time limit within which it must be delivered.
Designated Officers: Officers responsible for providing the services are clearly identified for each department.
Grievance Redressal Mechanism: Citizens can file a complaint if services are not provided on time. The grievance will be escalated to higher authorities for resolution.
Penalties: If services are delayed beyond the specified timeline, fines and penalties can be imposed on the responsible officer.
Benefits for Citizens
Enhanced Accountability: The act empowers citizens to hold public officials accountable for delayed or incomplete services.
Quick Resolution: The time-bound mechanism ensures faster service delivery.
Awareness and Empowerment: Citizens are informed of their rights and the services they are entitled to receive.
How SRLM Implements Janhit Guarantee Adhiniyam
Under SRLM (State Rural Livelihood Mission), services related to livelihoods, skill development, self-employment, and rural development are aligned with the provisions of the Janhit Guarantee Adhiniyam.

Ensuring beneficiaries receive timely training, financial aid, and support.
Reducing delays in project approvals, fund disbursements, and other critical services.
Promoting transparency in government schemes through regular updates and public notices on the official website.
