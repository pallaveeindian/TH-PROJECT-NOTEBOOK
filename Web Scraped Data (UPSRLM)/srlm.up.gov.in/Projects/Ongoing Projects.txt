Livelihoods Promotion for SHGs:
Project Title: Strengthening SHGs through Livelihood Support Initiatives
Objective: Enhance income generation for SHGs by promoting agro-processing, handicrafts, and food processing industries.
Key Activities:
Skill development training for SHG members.
Market linkages for SHG products.
Financial assistance and subsidies.
Digital Inclusion for Rural Communities:
Project Title: Digital Literacy and Financial Inclusion Drive
Objective: Empower rural communities with digital and financial literacy skills.
Key Activities:
Training on mobile banking, UPI, and online payments.
Setting up village information centers for digital access.
RSETI (Rural Skill Development Project):
Project Title: Rural Self Employment Training Initiative
Objective: Provide vocational training to rural youth for self-employment.
Key Activities:
Free vocational training programs.
Business mentorship and financial linkages.
Future Projects
Agro-Entrepreneurship Development Project:
Objective: Promote sustainable agriculture and agro-entrepreneurship among SHGs and rural youth.
Planned Activities:
Training on organic farming, beekeeping, and dairy management.
Creation of farmer producer organizations (FPOs).
Linkage to export markets.
Rural Women’s Empowerment Program:
Objective: Foster women’s empowerment through skill development and social awareness programs.
Planned Activities:
Self-defense training and legal literacy workshops.
Women-centric financial literacy drives.
Setting up women-owned rural enterprises.
Renewable Energy for Rural Livelihoods:
Objective: Promote the use of renewable energy to power rural livelihood activities.
Planned Activities:
Installation of solar-powered cold storage for farmers.
Solar energy adoption in rural dairy units.
Awareness campaigns on sustainable energy.
Youth Employment Initiative:
Objective: Provide comprehensive career counseling and job placement services to rural youth.
Planned Activities:
Career counseling workshops.
Vocational training in IT, construction, and healthcare sectors.
Partnerships with private companies for job placement.
