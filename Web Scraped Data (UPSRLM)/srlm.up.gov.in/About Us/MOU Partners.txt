NABARD - Financial (F) and Capacity Building (NF)
Typical Contribution Roles:
Financial (F): These partners provide loans, financial inclusion services, and credit linkages to SHGs (Self-Help Groups) supported by UPSRLM.
Non-Financial (NF): Some partners may also provide capacity-building programs, financial literacy workshops, and digital financial services training.
